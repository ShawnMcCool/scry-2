Win/Loss Markers on the Rank Line

Three approaches — from minimal to information-dense. Currently wins/losses are a separate bar chart below the line.

A: Colored Dots (Recommended)

Replace the uniform indigo dots with green for wins and orange for losses. Remove the separate results bar chart entirely. Minimal change — the line shape tells the rank story, dot colors tell win/loss.

B: Colored Segments + Dots

Color the line segments themselves — green segments for wins (rank goes up), orange segments for losses (rank drops). Plus colored dots. More information density but busier visually. Loses the clean indigo identity.

C: Directional Markers

Keep the indigo line. Use ▲ green up-triangles for wins and ▼ orange down-triangles for losses. Shape encodes direction, color encodes outcome. Most distinctive but slightly heavier visually.
