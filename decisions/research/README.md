# Research Records

Research records document multi-avenue investigations that inform — but don't yet commit to — architectural decisions. They follow the MADR 4.0 structure but their status is `exploratory` rather than `accepted`. The output of a research record is usually a shortlist of options that a subsequent architecture ADR will choose among.

**Filename convention:** `YYYY-MM-DD-NNN-short-title.md` — sequential numbering within this category.
